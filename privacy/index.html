Privacy Policy

At Haller Apps, we take user privacy extremely seriously. Select the policy below to see how we protect your data on our website and inside our apps.

1. General Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Haller Apps ("we", "our", "us") handles data collected via our website (https://hallerapps.com). We are committed to maintaining a transparent relationship with our users.

2. Information We Collect

Our website is a static showcase page. We do not require users to create accounts, and we do not collect, process, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) when you browse this site.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We do not place tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or use server-side tracking on this website. Our hosting provider may maintain basic server access logs (containing standard web information like IP addresses and user agents) purely for security diagnostics and traffic load maintenance, which are automatically rotated and deleted.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external app stores (like the Google Play Store) or other third-party websites. Once you redirect from our website, their respective privacy terms and cookie settings apply.

5. Contacting Us

If you contact us via email (hallerappsinfo@gmail.com) for customer support or feedback, we will only use your email address and any shared message details to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell or share your contact details.

QR Code Scanner & PDF Scan Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users of the QR Code Scanner app. All scanning operations are run client-side on your device.

The application only performs the following actions locally on your device:

  • Scanning QR codes and traditional barcodes.
  • Generating custom QR code graphics.
  • Cropping, filtering, and organizing image scans into PDF files.

These operations occur entirely on your device and are not sent to any external server.

2. Use of Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (a mobile application diagnostics tool provided by Google) to understand usage trends and improve general stability. Firebase Analytics collects non-personal, aggregated usage statistics, which may include:

  • Device type and model.
  • Installed app version.
  • Basic system events (crashes, interface interactions).

Firebase Analytics does not collect personal identifiers, nor does it have access to scanned QR code texts or camera visual frames. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any aggregated usage logs or other app-related metrics with third-party companies, advertising networks, or individuals outside of Google Firebase services mentioned above.

4. QR Code & Scan Content

The data contained within QR codes you scan or generate:

  • Remains strictly stored locally on your device storage.
  • Is never uploaded to any cloud infrastructure (we do not run proprietary database servers for this app).
  • Is never analyzed for profiling purposes.

5. Permissions Required

To perform its core functions, the application may request:

  • Camera Permission: Required solely to capture QR codes via the physical lens. The app does not record, upload, or transmit any video footage or camera frames.
  • Storage Access (optional): Required only if you select a picture from your local device gallery to scan for codes or choose to export PDF files to your local files storage.

No other permissions are requested or required to run the application.

6. Support Contacts

For policy inquiries or support assistance, contact us at: hallerappsinfo@gmail.com
