Before Contacting Us

To help us solve your query as quickly as possible, please verify the following details:

  • Ensure you are running the latest app version from the Google Play Store.
  • Describe the issue in detail (steps to reproduce, what goes wrong).
  • If possible, specify your Android device model and OS version.
  • Attach screenshots or screen recordings if applicable.
